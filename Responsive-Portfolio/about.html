About Me

Originally from northern Minnesota, from a town (village) of 600 people. Went to college in Duluth, MN and graduated with a BS degree in biology and chemistry minor. Moved to Denver, CO in 2010 and became certified as a clinical laboratory scientist in 2013. Started working at the University of Colorado Hospital in their Molecular and Virology Department shortly after.

Moved to Austin, TX in 2015 and started working as a Corporate Quality Manager for Sonic Healthcare USA, one of the largest clinical laboratory networks in the world. In 2016, transitioned to work for Sonic Reference Laboratory as their Quality Manager which is one of their newest and latest esoteric laboratories in the US. In 2016, joined the UT Coding BootCamp to make a transition into the web development world!
